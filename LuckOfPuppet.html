Deep within the world of science, scientists were gearing up for an exciting experiment with cutting-edge puppets. These puppets were programmed to complete challenging tasks by navigating predefined courses. However, the scientists were interested in testing the puppets' decision-making abilities and creativity. That's why they constructed specially designed courses. Each puppet had to make choices from randomly assigned features before entering the course.

Once on the course, the puppets' use of these chosen features was observed with bated breath. As the puppets showcased their skills and intelligence, scientists watched carefully every move. The pivotal moment arrived at the end of the course when the puppet had to choose a door. These door selections played a significant role in determining the fate of the next test.

However, before the experiments could begin, an unexpected event occurred. One of the tubes holding the puppets broke, and a puppet escaped from its chamber before the experiments could begin. The puppet had to reach the exit door after passing through the challenging test courses in order to reach freedom. One of the puppets had escaped, and an exciting adventure had begun.

Your adventure begins now. Go puppet, if you're lucky, you can succeed!
